    Children and Youth

    Growing young hearts and minds in faith, friendship, and fun

    Nurturing the Next Generation

    At St Chad's Handforth, we believe that children and young people are not just the future of our church - they're an essential part of our community today. Our vibrant children's and youth ministries provide safe, fun, and engaging environments where young people can explore faith, build friendships, and discover their unique gifts and calling.

    Our Age Groups

    Little Lambs

    Ages 0-3

    A gentle introduction to church for our youngest members and their parents, with sensory play, simple songs, and Bible stories.

    Sundays 10:30 AM
    Parent & child together
    Safe, nurturing environment

    Sunbeams

    Ages 4-7

    Creative Bible stories, craft activities, games, and worship songs designed for curious young minds learning about God's love.

    Sundays 10:30 AM
    Crafts & activities
    Fun worship songs

    Bright Stars

    Ages 8-11

    Interactive Bible exploration, team challenges, drama, and discussions that help children understand their faith in practical ways.

    Sundays 10:30 AM
    Bible exploration
    Interactive challenges

    Ignite

    Ages 12-15

    Relevant discussions about faith and life, creative worship, community service projects, and building strong friendships.

    Sundays 6:00 PM
    Real-life discussions
    Service projects

    Blaze

    Ages 16-18

    Leadership development, deep theological discussions, mission trips, and preparation for adult faith and responsibility.

    Sundays 6:00 PM
    Leadership training
    Mission opportunities

    Connect

    Ages 18-25

    Young adults navigating university, work, and relationships while building a strong foundation for lifelong faith and community.

    Wednesdays 7:30 PM
    Informal atmosphere
    Peer support

    Weekly Programs

    Tuesday Tots

    When: Tuesdays, 9:30 AM - 11:30 AM

    Who: Parents/carers with children 0-4

    A friendly playgroup with toys, crafts, songs, and stories. Great coffee and homemade cakes for adults!

    £3 per family includes refreshments

    Friday Fun Club

    When: Fridays, 6:00 PM - 7:30 PM

    Who: Children ages 5-11

    Games, crafts, Bible stories, and snacks in a fun, energetic environment. Perfect for after school wind-down!

    £2 per child includes snacks

    Youth Alpha

    When: Saturdays, 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM

    Who: Ages 14-18

    Explore the big questions of life and faith in a relaxed setting with food, videos, and open discussions.

    8-week course, runs twice yearly

    Sleepover Nights

    When: Once monthly, Friday evenings

    Who: Ages 8-15 (separate age groups)

    Games, movies, midnight snacks, and morning devotions. A favorite with kids and parents alike!

    £15 includes all meals and activities

    Special Events & Activities

    Summer Camp

    Annual week-long adventure camp with outdoor activities, team challenges, and evening celebrations.

    Holiday Clubs

    Easter and Christmas themed weeks packed with crafts, drama, games, and festive fun.

    Mission Trips

    International and UK-based mission opportunities for teens to serve others and grow in faith.

    Birthday Celebrations

    Monthly birthday parties for all our children and youth with games, cake, and special prayers.

    Christmas Nativity

    Annual nativity play where every child gets a part, from angels to shepherds to wise men.

    Sports Day

    Inter-church sports tournament with medals for everyone and lots of family fun.

    Safety & Safeguarding

    The safety and wellbeing of all our children and young people is our top priority. We are committed to providing a safe, nurturing environment where everyone can thrive.

    Our Commitment:
    • All leaders are DBS checked
    • Safeguarding training for all staff
    • Safe supervision ratios
    • First aid trained leaders
    Policies:
    • Comprehensive safeguarding policy
    • Open door policy
    • Clear communication with parents
    • Inclusive environment for all

    For more information about our safeguarding policies, please speak to our Children's Ministry Leader.

    Get Involved

    For Families

    Bring your children to any of our programs - no need to book, just turn up! We'd love to welcome your family into our community.

    • All children welcome regardless of background
    • Special provision for children with additional needs
    • Family events throughout the year
    • Support for parents and carers

    For Volunteers

    Whether you're great with toddlers or teenagers, we need enthusiastic volunteers to help shape young lives and have fun doing it!

    • Full training and ongoing support provided
    • DBS check assistance
    • Flexible commitment levels
    • Join a fantastic team of volunteers
