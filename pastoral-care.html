Pastoral Care

Supporting our community with compassion and care

Our Heart for Pastoral Care

At St Chad's Handforth, we believe that caring for one another is at the very heart of Christian community. Our pastoral care ministry is dedicated to providing support, comfort, and practical help to all members of our church family and the wider community.

Emotional Support

We provide a listening ear and emotional support during difficult times, offering prayer, encouragement, and practical guidance through life's challenges.

Practical Help

From meals during illness to transport for appointments, our caring team offers practical assistance to help you through challenging periods.

Home Visits

Our pastoral team and trained volunteers visit those who are housebound, in hospital, or simply need companionship and fellowship.

Prayer Ministry

We offer confidential prayer support for any situation. Our prayer team is available to pray with you and for you during times of need.

Need Pastoral Care?

If you or someone you know would benefit from pastoral care, please don't hesitate to reach out. All requests are treated with complete confidentiality.
