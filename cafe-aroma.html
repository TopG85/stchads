Café Aroma

Where community meets over great coffee and warm conversation

Welcome to Café Aroma

Café Aroma is more than just a coffee shop - it's the heart of our community. Whether you're looking for a quiet place to work, a friendly chat with neighbors, or simply want to enjoy excellent coffee and homemade treats, you'll find a warm welcome here.

What Makes Us Special

Premium Coffee

Locally roasted, ethically sourced coffee beans brewed to perfection by our trained baristas.

Homemade Treats

Fresh cakes, pastries, and light meals made daily by our volunteer baking team.

Community Spirit

A welcoming space where everyone belongs, fostering friendship and community connections.

Social Purpose

All profits support our community outreach programs and church ministries.
