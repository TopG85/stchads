Prayer and Worship

Connecting with God through prayer, praise, and fellowship

The Heart of Our Community

At St Chad's Handforth, prayer and worship are at the very center of everything we do. We believe that gathering together to praise God, pray for one another, and study His word strengthens our faith and builds our community.

Worship Services

Sunday Morning Service

10:30 AM

Our main weekly gathering featuring contemporary worship, biblical teaching, and communion. A warm, welcoming service for all ages.

Duration: 1 hour 15 minutes
All ages welcome
Children's activities provided

Evening Prayer

6:00 PM (1st Sunday)

A quieter, more contemplative service featuring traditional hymns, readings, and reflective prayer time.

Duration: 45 minutes
Contemplative style
Traditional liturgy

Midweek Prayer

Wednesday 7:00 PM

An intimate gathering focused on prayer, Bible study, and mutual encouragement. Everyone welcome.

Duration: 1 hour
Intimate setting
Bible study included

Prayer Ministry

Prayer Team

Our dedicated prayer team is available before and after services to pray with anyone who needs support, healing, or guidance.

Prayer Requests

Submit prayer requests confidentially through our prayer box or speak to any member of our pastoral team.

24/7 Prayer Chain

Urgent prayer needs are shared with our prayer chain team who commit to pray immediately and throughout the week.

Prayer Room

Our quiet prayer room is always available for personal prayer and reflection during church opening hours.

Join Our Worship Team

Musicians & Singers Welcome!

We're always looking for passionate musicians and singers to join our worship team. Whether you're experienced or just starting out, there's a place for you.

Instruments We Need:
  • Piano/Keyboard
  • Guitar (acoustic & electric)
  • Bass guitar
  • Drums
  • Vocals
What We Offer:
  • Supportive team environment
  • Regular practice sessions
  • Music training opportunities
  • Close fellowship
  • Spiritual growth
